Our Lead Designer

GharYogya Interiors by Binny is a premier interior design company dedicated to crafting aesthetically stunning and highly functional spaces. Our mission is to transform your house into a home that truly reflects your personality, lifestyle, and aspirations.

At the heart of GharYogya Interiors is Binny, a visionary designer with an exceptional blend of technical expertise and artistic sensibility. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering and a Master’s degree in Infrastructure Engineering, equipping her with deep knowledge of structural integrity, space planning, and home functionality. Complementing this, her Diploma in Interior Design from AAFT adds a creative edge, allowing her to infuse artistic elegance into every project. This rare combination of engineering precision and design artistry enables Binny to create spaces that are not just beautiful but also structurally sound and supremely livable.

At GharYogya Interiors, we don’t just design interiors—we create harmonious, intelligent, and inspiring living environments tailored to your needs.
